ABOUT

Being a Toastmaster and a avid techie, I have come up with a innovative solution that will solve most of the problems faced in the TMI fraternity using technology. For this, I want to propose a concept for a app which will perform functions wil make a lot of processes in a Toasmaster meeting efficient.

WHAT WILL THE APP CONSIST OF?

DigiAwards

Living in the era of technology, DigiAwards aims to reduce the amount of money spent on trophies by giving users DigiAwards. DigiAwards is a virtual award awarded to Toastmasters based on the category they have excelled in. This can later be shared on other platforms such as LinkedIn, etc.

Tracker

The Tracker option will allow Toastmasters to track their progress in their journey of Toastmasters.

Roles

The Roles feature will allow to Toastmaster to recieve updates on speaking slots and roles avalaible for upcomimg meetings and give them the option to book a slot.

Voting

The Voting feature allows Toastmasters to vote for the best speaker in the respective category. This feature will have many benefits and efficient test cases as in a converntial Toastmasters meeting is the traditional paper ballot and with this feature, lots of cost will saved on paper and time spent on counting the votes will be reduced.
